Features Built for
AI Creators

Everything you need to organize and manage your AI workflow in one beautiful, local-first app.

Image Prompts

Store and organize all your image generation prompts in one place. Add thumbnails, tags, model names, and organize into folders. Switch between card and list views to find what you need fast.

  • Card and list view modes
  • Custom thumbnails for quick identification
  • Tag and folder organization
  • Favorite prompts for quick access
  • Bulk actions for efficiency

Automatic Metadata Extraction

Drop in images generated from ComfyUI or Automatic1111 and automatically extract all the metadata—prompts, models, samplers, steps, CFG scale, and more. Turn any image into a reusable prompt with one click.

  • Supports ComfyUI and A1111 workflows
  • Extract positive and negative prompts
  • Capture model names and parameters
  • "Create Prompt" converts images to prompts
  • Preserve your workflow history

System Prompts & Character Cards

Manage your LLM system prompts and character cards with personality. Add profile images, organize by use case, and keep your AI personas ready to go. Perfect for ChatGPT, Claude, and local LLMs.

  • Profile images with thumbnail generation
  • Store instructions and personality traits
  • Tag by model (GPT-4, Claude, Llama, etc.)
  • Organize personas by category
  • Quick copy to clipboard

Conversation Library

Import and archive your AI conversations. Keep a library of your chats from ChatGPT, Claude, or any LLM. Search, tag, and organize your conversation history forever.

  • Import JSON, TXT, and Markdown exports
  • Tag and categorize conversations
  • Search through conversation content
  • Local backup of all your chats
  • Never lose valuable AI interactions

Model Library

Index your Stable Diffusion checkpoints, LoRAs, VAEs, and LLM models without moving or copying files. Link folders and ArtifactKeep automatically tracks everything.

  • Index models in-place (no file copying)
  • Support for SD checkpoints, LoRAs, VAEs
  • LLM model tracking
  • Automatic file size and type detection
  • Quick search across all models

Privacy & Local-First

Everything stays on your machine. No cloud sync. No tracking. No external servers. Your prompts, images, and data are yours alone. Export anytime in JSON, Markdown, CSV, or TXT.

  • 100% offline functionality
  • No telemetry or analytics
  • Open database format (SQLite)
  • Export in multiple formats
  • Move your data anytime
